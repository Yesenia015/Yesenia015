Episode 1: Retail Meets the Rich
We meet college students working in retail catering to the rich. Despite being avid city lovers, we break down the struggles of working in retail in a populated rich area known as Chelsea.





Episode 2: The Rent Money
Jane and Irma are roommates and the rent has come due. It should be no problem, as they've been on a new budget. But when Irma's friend Alice stops by with an attrative gambling tip Irma must decide who's more urgent, Alice, or the landlady.
